Fuel Cost Calculator

In this fuel cost calculator, you can check and compare the monthly running cost. You can compare the daily, monthly and yearly fuel cost of one petrol, diesel or CNG car with other car of a different fuel type. You only need to enter the petrol, diesel or CNG cost per unit or per litre and the approximate mileage you expect from the vehicle.

The latest fuel price will come automatically based on the selected city. You can also change the city to get the latest petrol, diesel, and CNG fuel prices for your city. While calculating the daily, monthly or yearly fuel cost of your car, you can also manually enter higher or lower fuel prices. This will help you estimate the running cost of your petrol, diesel or CNG vehicle if the fuel prices go up because of inflation or higher crude oil prices.
